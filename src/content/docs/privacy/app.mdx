_Last updated: May 24th 2025_

Welcome to Crossonic. We are committed to respecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy sets out how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Crossonic app.

If you have any questions regarding this policy, you may reach us at: <a href="mailto:contact@crossonic.org">contact@crossonic.org</a>

## Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Crossonic app ("we", "our", or "us") and covers our collection, use, and disclosure of your information.
This Policy does not cover any third-party websites, services, or applications that can be accessed through our app,
or third-party services you may access through Immich.

## Information We Collect

Crossonic stores your login details (_server url_, _username_, _password_, _api key_) in encrypted form locally on-device.
Additionally, your settings, cached versions of album/artist/playlist images and the contents of downloaded playlists (_media files_, _metadata_) are stored locally on your device.

We do not have access to this data, nor do we transmit or store it on any of our servers.

## Connection to Music Servers

When you connect to a music server that server gains access to your connection details including your IP address. To authenticate your requests
Crossonic sends your login details (_username_, _password_ and/or _api key_) to the server.

When you change any playlist cover your selected image is uploaded to the connected music server.

Whether this information is encrypted in transit depends
on how your connect to the server. If you connect to the server over _HTTPS_, all communication data is securely encrypted with TLS.

Make sure that you only connect to music servers you trust with this data and make sure to read their privacy policy.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.
If we make any changes, we will notify you by revising the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
It's encouraged that users frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about what information we collect and how we are helping to protect it.
